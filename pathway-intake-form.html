New Pathway to Success — Creation Request

This form initiates the Pathway creation pipeline. Your answers directly feed Claude's content generation, the misconception library, and the AI coach scaffolding. The more detail you provide, the more precisely the Pathway reflects EDGE's Know / Show / Grow philosophy.

What happens after you submit: n8n receives this form, passes it to Claude, and generates the full Pathway Package — KSG-structured content, misconception library entries, My Goals prompt, practice problems, and Tutor Kit notes. You'll review the output in Google Sheets before anything moves to Pictory or Coursebox.
